St.James Outreach Website

Created a website for St.James Outreach where volunteers can store guest information which could then be pulled up and/or editted at a later time. This website is also used to record what type, when, and the amount of help that was given to a specific guest as well as provide an overview of help given of a selected date range. This website was create by a group of 4 developers in a Agile format with 2 week sprints.
